Meliora

A small and cozy town surrounded by nature in Upstate New York. It was established in 1798 and has a population of 1,125. It is well known for its flower festivals and local production of honey used in many baked goods and sweets. Tourists would sometimes go to Meliora for a quick getaway from the busy cities. Mayor Andreas Diamandis currently runs the town and is always working hard to make the town a peaceful place for everyone to enjoy. "Better times await!" is the town slogan that shows both visitors and residents that being in Meliora is the start of many good things to come.

Key Locations

Petal Lane

A vast and cozy residential area for the beloved townspeople.

  • Flores Home - The Flores family had rough history with a strained relationship between Cecilia and her daughter, Maya who immediately left at 18. Cecilia would reside in the house in lonesome until her death. Maya moves back to the house with her son, Kai, after so many years.
  • Valencia Home - The Valencia is said to be an ideal family with the loving couple, Oscar and Selena Valencia and their twin kids, Luis and Lucia.
  • Campbell Home - The Campbell family is known for being very active in the Community Garden, especially the youngest daughter and talented gardener, Millie.
  • Beckett-Diamandis Family Manor - Originally owned by the Beckett family until the marriage of Andreas Diamandis and Melissa Beckett. The Diamandis family currently reside in the manor, which is just Mayor Andreas and his only child, Pamela.

Blossom Street

The main street where most stores can be found as well as some historical sites. It is fairly popular with tourists.

  • Meliora Town Hall - The place where Mayor Andreas Diamandis works to come up with new ways to improve the town.
  • Meliora Prep School - A K-12 prep school that the majority of the kids in town attend.
  • Meliora Library - One of the oldest buildings in town with a wide selection of books and archives on the town's history. The librarian Felix Sommer is a kind individual who loves to converse with people about almost any topic.
  • Mariposa Antique Shop - A small antique shop currently owned by the mysterious Vanessa Reynoso who moved to town a few years ago.
  • Valencia Detective Agency - A private detective agency owned by Oscar Valencia that is focused on pursuing justice and the innocent's safety.
  • Meliora Community Garden - A place that encourages the townspeople to connect with nature and come together with various community events.

Forest

A vast forest surrounding the town that is popular for camping trips and exploration.

  • Vanessa's Cottage - A small cottage that appeared when Vanessa Reynoso moved to Meliora. Not many people visit the place as it is a bit far down the woods.
  • Crystal Lake - A lake in the forest popular for swimming by some residents and tourists. However, rumors are that something sinister lives there that only appears at night.
  • Lovette Manor - An abandoned manor that mysteriously burnt down in 1978, killing the Lovette family. It is said to be haunted by the spirits of some of the family members.
